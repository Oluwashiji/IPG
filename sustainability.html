Our Sustainability Journey

Shaping a better future through responsible packaging, ethical sourcing, and planet-friendly goals.

Better, Every Day: Our Sustainability Pillars

Better Packaging

Innovating sustainable paper-based packaging, reducing plastics, and creating recyclable solutions like Boardio™, CleanClose™, and EnviroClip™.

Better for People

We prioritize safety, diversity, equity, inclusion, and investing in our employees and local communities worldwide.

Better Future

We're targeting net-zero emissions by 2050 with science-based climate goals, renewable energy, and environmental leadership.

2030 & 2050 Sustainability Goals

  • 🌍 Replace ~1 billion plastic packages with recyclable fiber-based solutions by 2030.
  • 🌱 Ensure 97% of packaging is recyclable, renewable, or compostable.
  • ⚡ Source 100% renewable electricity in EMEA facilities.
  • 🏭 Achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2050, with interim 2030 targets.
  • 📦 Maintain 89%+ certified fiber sourcing from sustainably managed forests.
  • 🎓 Enhance diversity, equity & inclusion through leadership programs and community outreach.
